Dear Geffrey,

Happy 19th! Does this mean we can legally go for a drink (almost) anywhere now? You've become such an important person in my life that I don't know where to even begin to express my love and gratitude for you; but I will take Geoffrey Day as a chance to try. I guess there are three parts to my lil confession.


Firstly, I'm sorry. I'm sorry for making you do all the tasks and make the decisions that I don't wanna. And for forcing you to take daily photos for my enjoyment (I don't think you realize how closely I analyze themüôÉ). For all the tickling you can't handle. I'm sorry for joking about belonging to so many others, when we both know I'm yours. But also, I'm sorry for taking less initiative and not being very yappy; I am still learning to fully opening up to you. I'm sorry for my brain shutdowns when I don't want to interact with people, or can not catch up to your thoughts.


But I also need to tell you thank you. Thank you for putting up with my silliness and my sadness. Thank you for being such a good boyfriend who is willing to listen and apologize, to be transparent and patient. Thank you for putting trust in me and letting me be the one you ramble to. Thank you for letting me knock your rock and cuddling me, and making me feel loved. Thank you for making me want to be a better version of me. Thank you for believing in me, always.


Lastly...I love you. I love listening to you talk about something you're passionate about, or watching you try to figure out a task. I love how you will walk on the outside of the sidewalk. I love how fluffy your hair is right after a shower, or how cute you are when you wanna stay in bed in the mornings. I love how you'll always ask for my hand, or how you're always looking for a kiss. I love your ambition and drive. I love that you give me butterflies when you get too close or say something sweet. I love (and hate) your pick up lines and scary winks. Most importantly, I love knowing you're mine.


I'm excited to spend another year with you. Happy 19th. I promise the next letter I write will be in pretty calligraphy.


Yours truly,

Chloe
